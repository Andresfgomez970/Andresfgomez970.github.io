About Vakoika

Who we are

Vakoika unlocks the possibilities of how we enjoy vacation homes. We take care of managing our homeowners’ vacation houses so they can actually enjoy their free time (and their home when they want to). And our guests book vacations with peace of mind, knowing they’re going to find exactly what they’re looking for without any surprises.

With industry-leading technology, we help people invest smarter—from where to buy a property, to which amenities will earn five-star reviews. It all works together to maximize revenue for our homeowners.

At vakoika, our people are leading the charge. With our professional, local caretaking teams in every market we manage, central support staff, and 24/7 customer service, we’ll be there every step of the way.

Vakoika Meaning

Vakoika is a combination of the word Vakansies from the african languague, which mean holidays, and the word Oikia from the greek, which means family and home. As a result, the first part of our name, Vak, from the african, represents the nomadic origin of our species. That nomadic nature remains with us and we consider it an inseparable part of humans, associated for our desire of freedom. On the other hand, Oika stands for order an stability. We believe that our name represent the balance that stive to achive in our vacational homes.
