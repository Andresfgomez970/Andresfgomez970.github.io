Waldorf Salad

Description

Ingredients

This delicios salad has an unquie combination of flavors that makes it simple and of very good flavor for many ocassions. Its mayo and Djon mustard, accompanied with apple and cabagge gives it a perfect combination of textures and flavors. Apart from the flavor it is good for visits since the apples, celery, and grapes can all be green, which harmonizes the color palette of the dish. We recommend to eat this course as a light meal, or to eat it with a good meat and and smash potatoes.

  • 6 tablespoons mayonnaise (or plain yogurt)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Pinch freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 sweet apples, cored and chopped
  • 1 cup seedless red grapes, halved, or 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • 1 cup chopped, slightly toasted walnuts
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. In a medium sized bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise (or yogurt), lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  2. Add the apple, celery, grapes, and walnuts
  3. Stir the apple, celery, grapes, and walnuts into the bowl with the dressing.
  4. Spoon salad onto a bed of fresh lettuce and serve.
