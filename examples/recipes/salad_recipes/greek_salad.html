Greek Salad

Description

Ingredients

This delicios salad has an unquie combination of flavors that makes it a unique experience. This remarkable salad has a sour flavor, thaks to its cheese, that is perfectly balance by the rest of the ingredients. The cherry tomatoes provide a juiciy taste, while the onion contrast with its stiffness and the absorbed wine vinegar. In the middle of this textures we have the cucumber. Finally, the Kalamata olives gives the last touch. We recommend to accompany this course with fresh fish and a good wine.

Steps

Toss the ingredient as it is indicated in the following steps:
  1. 2 cups halved cherry tomatoes.
  2. 1 English cucumber, cut lengthwise, seeded, and sliced ¼-inch thick
  3. Green bell pepper, chopped into 1-inch pieces
  4. 5 ounces feta cheese crumbled by the hand, this gives irregularity to the pieces and gives a better flavor as a result.
  5. 1/3 cup thinly sliced red onion after it was submerged for about 3 min in a mixture of half white wine vinager and half water.
  6. 1/3 cup of half sliced Kalamata olives
  7. 1/4 cup of extra-virgin olive oil
  8. 1 garlic clove, minced
  9. 1 / 2 teaspoon dried oregano, more for sprinkling
  10. 1 / 4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  11. 1 / 4 teaspoon sea salt
  12. Freshly ground black pepper to the taste
